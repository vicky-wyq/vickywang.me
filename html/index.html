Business owners managing appointments for their clients.

  • Decreased errors from 15 to 5 per 100 bookings, resulting in 67% fewer booking errors.

Enhancing the Consumer Service Booking Experience

Mastering B2B SaaS Subscription Success

It explores the challenges faced, the solutions implemented, and the significant impact these changes had on user satisfaction and conversion rates, ultimately driving subscription success in a competitive market.

B2B SaaS Booking App Revamp: The Transition from White-Glove to Self-Service

Discover how we transformed the pet booking app with a strategic redesign, shifting from a white-glove service to a user-friendly self-service platform. This overhaul focused on enhancing key features, resulting in a significant 45% increase in user satisfaction and a more streamlined experience for pet owners. Join us on our journey to revolutionize pet care services with a seamless and intuitive booking experience.

Shifting Focus: Transforming Local and Telehealth Services into RX Zero

This case study delves into LASO Health's strategic pivot towards the RX Zero model, moving away from RX Local and Telemedicine services. It highlights the key objectives of RX Zero, centering on credibility and affordability. The study showcases the streamlined onboarding and subscription process, along with a simplified ordering system.

RX Zero Savings Calculator: Uncover Prescription Meds Savings

LASO Admin Panel: From Concept to Reality - Simplified User Management

The core focus of this panel was to efficiently manage a diverse range of user accounts, including B2B, D2C, and guest users. A unique aspect of this project was accommodating users from B2B with company sponsorship, adding another layer of complexity to the design process.

About Me

As a UX designer, I possess a diverse range of skills and have gained experience in startup environments. I am fully involved in every aspect of the design process and continuously aim to create seamless user experiences that align with business objectives. My approach emphasizes simplicity, using the fewest design elements necessary to address core issues effectively.

My fascination with craftsmanship extends beyond just UX. It is evident both in the art of crafting user-centric interfaces and in appreciating the finer details of everyday life.

I take pleasure in immersing myself in new technologies. Doing so not only fosters smoother collaboration with developers but also deepens my understanding of design, ultimately driving productivity.

My Journey

  • The Art of Drawing & Painting

    Art is my way of turning emotions and ideas into visual expression. It deepens my understanding of design and opens up new perspectives. Through color, composition, and other elements, art helps me create unique designs.

  • Architectural Design & Photography

    In design, my heart lies in a vibrant creative vision that marries creativity, innovation, and precision with out-of-the-box thinking. In architecture, I focus on creating functional and visually appealing buildings that meet client' needs, balance different elements, and achieve a harmonious aesthetic.

  • Web Implementation & Design System Design/Update

    Since 2020, I have been designing, implementing, and maintaining my website. During this process, I have learned a lot, including managing a design system with HTML, CSS, JS, and EJS. I continuously improve my JavaScript knowledge, monitor site traffic with tools like Posthog and Google Analytics.

  • User Experience Design

    I love UX because it empowers users in the design process. My goal is always to solve problems through feedback and data analysis. The short design and development cycle in UX turns all design dreams into reality.
