Design Process

Understanding Users and Products

I aim to deeply comprehend business objectives and product specifics, while keeping user goals and pain points at the forefront. Based on this understanding, I conceive and plan immersive user experiences.

Building Strong Cross-Functional Partnerships

I turn ambiguous projects into articulated plans, ensuring cross-functional alignment along the way. By collaborating with different teams, I enhance the value and validity of research.

Managing the Design Process

I oversee each phase of the design process, from planning research to delivering high-quality designs that simultaneously solve user problems, meet their needs, and achieve business goals. My focus is on maintaining a feedback loop to continuously improve the design, and I ensure that each phase has a clear deadline to keep the project on track.
