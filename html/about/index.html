Design Process

Understanding Users and Products

In my view, a deep understanding of both business goals and user needs is fundamental to UX design. By prioritizing these aspects, I'm able to create immersive user experiences that truly resonate. This dual focus is crucial in ensuring that my design projects have a meaningful impact. It also aids in the planning and prioritization process, ensuring that we focus on what matters most.

Building Strong Cross-Functional Partnerships

I transform ambiguous projects into articulated plans, ensuring cross-functional alignment throughout the process. By setting early deadlines and establishing feedback loops for each milestone, I encourage timely feedback and iterative improvement. Collaborating closely with various teams, I enhance both the value and validity of research, leading to more informed and impactful design decisions.

Managing the Design Process

Throughout each phase of the design process, from planning research to iterating designs and delivering high-quality final products, my focus is on maintaining a feedback loop to continuously refine and improve the design. I ensure that each phase has a clear deadline to keep the project on track. By documenting each iteration and evaluating the strengths and weaknesses of each iteration, I aim to build a more effective and impactful design.
