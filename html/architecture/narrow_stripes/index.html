NARROW STRIPES

Vicky Wang's 'Narrow Stripes' integrates architecture and landscape, crafting a design that enables fluid interaction between interior and exterior spaces. The use of a series of straps divides the space, providing an infrastructure that serves the nearby community. This approach fosters connectivity among occupants and with the natural surroundings, creating an environment that encourages interaction and engagement with both the community and the natural world.
