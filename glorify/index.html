Context

Glorify is a mobile application that offers a vast library of content designed to support users in their spiritual journey, particularly those who follow the Christian faith. The app provides a range of resources such as daily devotionals, scripture readings, prayer guides, and other faith-based content that users can access at their convenience. Glorify's content is designed to be immersive and engaging, with a focus on helping users deepen their spiritual connection and growth.

Opportunity

Despite its rich content, Glorify has been primarily a single-player experience, lacking the community engagement features that are integral to Christianity. This can limit the app's ability to fully meet the user's needs for social and collaborative experiences in their faith journey. To improve the user experience, I exploring ways to incorporate community engagement features that align with the values of Christianity.
