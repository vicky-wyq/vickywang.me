Main goal

The objective of this design system is to effectively organize all the components on the website, making them easy to locate and reuse. It also aims to categorize different types of elements, allowing for easy comparison of their visual effects.

Breakdown

  • Establish a responsive grid as the basis
  • Select a typography and color palette, and make adjustments based on user feedback and other relevant factors
  • Design and implement components, testing their behavior, and updating them as needed to ensure they work seamlessly within the system
  • Providing a usage demonstration helps to clarify the underlying logic of a system, making it easier to compare and improve upon
