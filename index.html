Design principles

Product

  • Design MVP, testing, launch, learn from customers > adding features & make it perfect

Flow and layout

  • Keep it less and simple, users don't need to spend any effort reading or thinking about how to reach their goals
  • Think about design and flow together when updating the layout. Focus on the information hierarchy. Always make sure Call-To-Action Button is the clearest. and the important contents are secondary. Testing the layout with the flow, not a single screen.
  • Thoughtfully choose UI components, such as card, list, table, accordion, tab, or combinations. Think about the information, what information users want to see, and what information users should see. For example, designing a business profile, with cards including images and descriptions. it will show a lot of information about one item, in comparison the list will show a list of items with less information. Choose the one the business need.

Design system

  • Work with Dev about the responsive grid and break point, understand how current design system
  • Unify the overall margin, padding, and border lines, keep it simple and consistent
  • A limited number of colors and font, primary color with several variants of brightness(a range of similar colors), grey 100-900, may have additional colors.
