Desired Working Environment

Creative & Collaborative

Looking for a place that has a creative and collaborative culture where sharing, helping and lifting others up is encouraged
Looking for a company that encourages me to think. Employees are empowered to innovate and work autonomously, without micro-managing or hand-holding.

Transparency

Workplaces with positive cultures support a philosophy of transparency so that every team member feels they know where they stand, where the company is headed, and in general they feel “in the loop".
I am always willing to know the product's short- and long-term goals. Understand the work while doing it.
