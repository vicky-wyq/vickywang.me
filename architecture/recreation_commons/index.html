RECREATION COMMONS

Studio:   ARCH 203 - Integrated Design Studio
Duration:  One semester | Fall 2018
Project:   M. Arch, CED, UC Berkeley
Site:   Site: 110 Franklin St. San Francisco
Scale:   Total Floor Area: 22900 s.f. 4 Floors
Instructor:   Roddy Creedon

Two volums create one spcace

The two volumes are designed to differentiate between programs for public access and those for private use in the Recreation Commons.

Programs

PRIVATE VS. PUBLIC

Integrate two volumes within a single space to differentiate between the main programs: one for public access and the other for private purposes.

EXERCISE VS. LEISURE
the circulation integrates the two volumes as one space. it creates a dynamic view between the leisure and training sections.
