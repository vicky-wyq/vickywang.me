Research

Challenge and target audience

The Yello Interview Scheduling Survey

On average, recruiters are spending ⅔ of their overall hiring time on the interview process.

67% of respondents say it takes between 30 minutes and 2 hours to schedule a single interview.

60% of recruiters say that they regularly lose candidates before they’re able to schedule an interview.

My goal is design a scheduling tool for recruiting interviews. it helps recruiters and hiring managers arrange and schedule interviews with less time and effort.

Recruiters and hiring managers usually invite colleagues to attend interviews. It takes time to match candidate and colleague's availability in different calendars and apps.

Matching colleagues' interview skills to candidate interview type from different teams sometimes is a challenge as well.

User journey

I learned through my research that scheduling interviews fundamentally is to find availability from interviewers and candidates

In order to have available interviews. the Interview skill set needs match interview type

Personas

From the hiring manager interview, I realized the challenge of scheduling interviews is team memebrs' availability and skills

From the recruiter interview, I found team availability and organizing interview schedule, notifying people are important steps to scheude an interview

User flows

The recruiter user flow

New users will set up their team and interview skills first

Afterwards, they will add interview types with required interview skills

Eventually, team members' interview skills and required interview skills will be matched automatically

Interview availability and capacity depend on how many available team members with the required skills for a type of interview

Insights

After usability testing and revisiting the user interview, the job interview process is most likely controlled by recruiters and recruiting coordinators. From the job interview to the final offer, they may have several interviews they are related, such as phone screens and technical interviews, the interviewers need to know the candidate interview status. Each interview should show the candidate's interview status and be able to link to the candidates' interview management website such as Greenhouse.

Key takeaways

Understand which data need to be matched in order to make the interview works.

For this website, matching candidates' availability with interviewers' availability is the key.

How to pair their availability, other than sync calendars and working hours, the interview skills play a significant role. Without matching candidates' and interveners' skills, the booking won't work.

I was trying to separate members into different teams, each group has their skills, but that will make this app less flexible but redundant
