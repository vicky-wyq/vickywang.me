Research

Challenge and target audience

My goal is design a scheduling tool for company recruiting interviews. it helps recruiters and hiring managers arrange and schedule interviews with less time and effort.

Recruiters and hiring managers usually invite colleagues to attend interviews. It takes time to find candidate and colleague's availability in different calendars and apps.

Matching colleagues' interview skills to candidate interview type from different team sometimes is a challenge as well.

Use cases

Hiring managers / Recruiters / HR
  • Invite team members to share availability
  • Invite candidate to schedule interview
  • Set team member interview skills
Team members
  • Share availability by connecting calendar
  • Connect to video conference account
  • Pass interview to another team member
Candidates
  • Schedule an interview via link
  • Provide information
  • Reschedule and cancel interview

User journey

I learned through my research that scheduling interviews fundamentally is to find availability from interviewers and candidates

In order to have available interviews. the Interview skill set needs match interview type

Personas

From the hiring manager interview, I realized the challenge of scheduling interviews is team memebrs' availability and skills

From the recruiter interview, I found team availability and organizing interview schedule, notifying people are important steps to scheude an interview

Insights

Team members' availability
  • Combine team members' availability
  • Schedule interviews base on availability
Team members' interview skills
  • Portfolio review
  • Phone screen
  • Frond end
Interview type
  • Based on candidate
  • Such as Mid-level front-end or Senior product manager

Checking colleagues' availability takes major time during scheduling an interview. team members are able to sync theircalendars , all the availability will be in one place easy to find by candidates

Hiring managers and recruiters define the interview type in this app. the candidate will be able to select a date and time and put some notes from a link that belongs to a type of the interview

Interview skills need to be trained and approved by the recruiter or hiring manager based on my interview. Interview skills will only add or modify in the admin account

User flows

The recruiter user flow

New users will set up their team and interview skills first

Afterwards, they will add interview types with required interview skills

Eventually, team members' interview skills and required interview skills will be matched automatically

Interview availability and capacity depend on how many available team members with the required skills for a type of interview
