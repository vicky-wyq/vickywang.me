Opportunity and use cases

My goal is to design a sentiment analysis tool to help the business owner grow their business.

The business sentiment analysis tool is a process of collecting and analyzing large volumes of data from social networks, websites, and other sources to determine what people think about a particular company.

Accompany want to know how people feel about a new feature launched, or a new item released.

They also want to understand the positive and negative experiences with the brand and products. understand the reason based on a bunch of data

Sentiment analysis helps e-commerce, marketing, advertising, politics, market research and more.

The customer support team is informed in real-time about any negative comments.

The mentions on social media get detected extremely quickly
