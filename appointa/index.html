The Outcome of a Discovery

Understand the challenge

On average, recruiters are spending two-thirds of their overall hiring process on interview scheduling.

Along with recruitment scheduling being very time-consuming, the following factors are the reasons for the difficulties:

  • Reach out and get availability from the candidate
  • Find an eligible interviewer from an interview pool, check availability
  • Match availability between qualified candidate and eligible interviewer
  • Easy to make mistakes, companies have to hire recruiter coordinators to make sure candidates won't have a bad experience
  • When booking an onsite interview, it is a huge challenge to find a group of people available at the same time

The recruitment app and scheduling app currently they are using are not very efficient for scheduling job interviews

The existing tools such as Greenhouse and Calendly schedule for a specific interviewer. Most time, finding an interviewer from an interview pool is unnecessary because it is a uniform predetermined interview.

Target user and market

The target user will be tech company recruiters and hiring managers. Ideally work in medium and large companies, with approximately more than 50 employees.

The recruiter user flow

Hiring managers and recruiters define the interview type in this app. the candidate will be able to select a date and time and put some notes from a link that belongs to a type of the interview

New users will set up their team and interview skills first

Afterwards, they will add interview types with required interview skills

Key takeaways

Understanding the use case and product goal is important in the beginning. Based on research, adjust the goal and design the MVP with minimal features

Understand how to match the data from both the interviewer and candidate sides

How to automate the interview scheduling part, let users achieve their goal with fewer steps and effort

Understand the concept of features. for example, the interview team and interview skills are related but not the same. Interview skills can be filled and matched along with interview type

It is very important for an early phase product to understand the goal and the concept of features, and make sure the feature I trying to build is aligned with the business goal.
