Challenge

The recruiter's primary focus is on finding qualified candidates for the open position. However, recruiters are spending two-thirds of their overall hiring process on interview scheduling on average.

Goal

Redefining the way recruiters arrange their appointments and invite attendees for medium/large-sized business

User research goals

What?   When?   Why?   How?  

What are the challenges of scheduling job interviews? When did the problem occur?

What are the types of situations that are most likely to spend longer time scheduling an interview?

What tools/methods are successful in scheduling job interviews, why do you need them?

How do the scheduling tools help the recruiters and recruiting coordinators to schedule job interviews for both interviewers and interviewees?

User flows

The recruiter user flow

Hiring managers and recruiters define the interview type in this app. the candidate will be able to select a date and time and put some notes from a link that belongs to a type of the interview

New users will set up their team and interview skills first

Afterwards, they will add interview types with required interview skills

Eventually, team members' interview skills and required interview skills will be matched automatically

Interview availability and capacity depend on how many available team members with the required skills for a type of interview

Key takeaways

Understanding the use case and product goal is important in the beginning. Based on research, adjust the goal and design the MVP with minimal features

Understand how to match the data from both the interviewer and candidate sides

How to automate the interview scheduling part, let users achieve their goal with fewer steps and effort

Understand the concept of features. for example, the interview team and interview skills are related but not the same. Interview skills can be filled and matched along with interview type

It is very important for an early phase product to understand the goal and the concept of features, and make sure the feature I trying to build is aligned with the business goal.
